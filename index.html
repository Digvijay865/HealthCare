Healthcare Tips

Eat Balanced Meals

Include fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins in your daily diet for optimal health and energy.

Stay Active

Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate exercise most days to boost heart health, mood, and immunity.

Get Quality Sleep

7–9 hours of sleep nightly helps your body repair, improves focus, and supports emotional well-being.

Manage Stress

Practice mindfulness, deep breathing, or journaling to reduce anxiety and improve mental clarity.
